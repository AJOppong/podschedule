Our Purpose

The Podcast Studio at the E-Learning Centre

The Podcast Studio at the E-Learning Centre, KNUST, was established to facilitate high-quality audio and video conversations around key topics affecting the university community. The E-Learning Centre runs a monthly podcast which hosts experts in diverse fields to talk about issues pertaining to the university community.

  • University updates (e.g. online orientation)
  • Student wellbeing (e.g. mental health, exam stress, time management)
  • Academic support and guidance from lecturers and experts
  • E-Learning related issues

Guidelines

Studio Etiquette

A premium recording requires a premium environment. Please observe these rules to ensure the best experience for everyone.

DO'S

  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled session to prep.
  • Bring scripts on a digital device or non-crinkly paper.
  • Keep hydrated (water only) prior to recording.
  • Communicate special requirements in advance.

DON'TS

  • Bring food or sticky drinks in the studio.
  • Avoid noisy jewelry or rustling clothing.
  • Do not adjust microphones without assistance.
  • Keep mobile phones on silent or airplane mode.
