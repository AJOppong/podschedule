Studio Etiquette

A high-quality recording starts with a professional environment. Please observe these guidelines.

DO'S

  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled session to prep equipment.
  • Bring scripts on a digital device or non-crinkly paper to avoid mic noise.
  • Drink water prior to recording to avoid dry mouth clicks.
  • Communicate any special technical requirements in advance.

DON'TS

  • Bring food or sticky drinks (like soda or juice) into the studio.
  • Wear noisy jewelry or clothing that rustles heavily.
  • Touch or adjust the microphones without the engineer's assistance.
  • Leave your mobile phones vibrating or ringing; set to silent mode.
